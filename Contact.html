FAQs

Email us
How quickly will I receive a response?
We strive to respond within 24–48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call emergency services.
Do you provide emergency support?
We’re not an emergency service. If you’re in immediate danger or experiencing a crisis, please call 911 (or your local emergency number) right away.
Can I volunteer with HATA Humanitarian?
Yes — we welcome volunteers. Send us a message with your interests and availability, and we’ll share current opportunities.
Are you available for speaking engagements?
Yes. We can participate in speaking engagements, workshops, and community events. Contact us with your date, audience, and topic.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions we receive.

How quickly will I receive a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call us directly.

Do you offer emergency support services?

While we provide various support programs, we are not an emergency service. If you are in crisis, please call 911 or reach out to specialized emergency services in our community.

Can I volunteer with HATA Humanitarian?

Yes! We welcome volunteers. Please indicate your interest in volunteering when you contact us, and we'll provide you with more information about current opportunities.

Are you available for speaking engagements?

Yes, our team members are available for speaking engagements, workshops, and community events. Please contact us with details about your event.

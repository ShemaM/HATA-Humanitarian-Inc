Our Guiding Principle

A Foundation of Inclusion & Belonging.

We believe every person deserves to be treated with dignity and respect. Our program focuses on empowering newcomers with essential skills and resources, ensuring they feel welcomed and equipped to build successful lives in their new community.

"We are committed to fostering inclusive communities where everyone feels seen, valued, and supported. Diversity is our strength, and belonging is our goal."

Our Services

How We Empower Newcomers.

Our work is grounded in the lived experiences of the communities we serve. We offer practical, hands-on support to help you navigate your new life in Winnipeg with confidence.

01

Language & Communication

Free language training (English & French) designed to improve your communication skills for daily life, employment, and building social connections.

02

Essential Skills Training

Practical workshops on digital literacy, financial management, and understanding Canadian workplace culture to boost your employability and independence.

03

Systems Navigation Support

One-on-one guidance to help you and your family navigate key Canadian systems, including healthcare, housing, and school registration for your children.

Addiction Recovery Program

Supporting youth on their journey to sobriety and wellness

Our Approach Get Help

Holistic Recovery Support

Our Addiction Recovery Program takes a comprehensive approach to supporting youth (ages 13-24) struggling with substance use. We combine evidence-based practices with peer support to address the root causes of addiction.

Need Immediate Help?

If you or someone you know is in crisis, call our 24/7 support line at 204-555-HELP.

Our Services

Addiction Education

Our educational programs help youth understand addiction and build healthy coping skills:

  • Weekly educational workshops
  • Science of addiction seminars
  • Harm reduction strategies
  • Healthy lifestyle training

All education is trauma-informed and delivered by professionals with lived experience.

Peer Support

We offer multiple support options tailored to youth needs:

  • Daily recovery meetings (in-person & virtual)
  • One-on-one mentoring
  • Family support groups
  • 24/7 crisis text line

All support groups are confidential and judgment-free.

Rehabilitation Partnerships

We collaborate with treatment centers to provide:

  • Referrals to youth-focused rehab programs
  • Scholarships for treatment
  • Transition planning
  • Family education during treatment

We maintain relationships with 5 local treatment centers specializing in youth addiction.

Aftercare Support

Continuing care is essential for lasting recovery:

  • Sober living referrals
  • Alumni events
  • Vocational training
  • Relapse prevention planning

Our aftercare program has demonstrated 75% sobriety rates at 12 months post-treatment.

Program Outcomes

Engagement

150+ youth actively participating in recovery programs each month, with 80% regular attendance.

Recovery

65% of participants maintain sobriety at 6 months, compared to 35% community average.

Education

90% of participants in our school reintegration program complete their academic year.

Employment

45% of alumni gain employment within 3 months of completing our vocational program.

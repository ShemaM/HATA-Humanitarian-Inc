About Us

HATA Humanitarian Inc. is a non-profit organization based in Winnipeg, Manitoba, dedicated to transforming lives through education, advocacy, and holistic support. Founded with a commitment to fostering resilience, inclusivity, and positive change, our organization empowers newcomers and other immigrants to overcome challenges and achieve their full potential.

By addressing critical issues such as mental health stigma, integration barriers, and substance addiction, we strive to create a thriving and compassionate community where everyone has the resources to succeed.

"Our vision is an inclusive community where every individual is empowered to thrive with resilience, dignity, and purpose."

Mission

To empower and transform lives by promoting mental well-being, fostering inclusivity, and supporting addiction recovery through education, advocacy, and community engagement.

Our Objectives

  1. Promote mental well-being and break the stigma surrounding mental health.

  2. Facilitate the integration of newcomers by providing essential skills training.

  3. Support youth struggling with addiction through food assistance and mindset education.

  4. Build a supportive network that encourages resilience, personal growth, and social inclusion.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every action we take and every life we touch.

Dignity & Respect

We believe every person, regardless of background or circumstance, deserves to be treated with dignity, compassion, and respect. We honour the unique journey of each individual and uphold their worth in every interaction.

Inclusion & Belonging

We are committed to fostering inclusive communities where everyone newcomers, youth, marginalized individuals feels seen, valued, and supported. Diversity is our strength, and belonging is our goal.

Integrity & Accountability

We act with transparency, honesty, and a strong sense of responsibility. Our decisions and actions reflect our mission, and we are accountable to the people and communities we serve.

Empowerment through Education

Knowledge transforms lives. We provide mindset education, life skills, and training to equip individuals with the tools they need to overcome challenges and shape their futures

Hope & Transformation

We believe in the power of hope to ignite change. From addiction recovery to newcomer integration, we walk alongside individuals on their journey to transformation and purpose.

Mental Well-being

We prioritize mental health and recovery by addressing stigma, encouraging resilience, and offering accessible, culturally sensitive support. We believe healing is a right, not a privilege.

Community-Centered Action

Our work is grounded in the lived experiences of the communities we serve. We engage collaboratively, build trust, and co-create solutions that bring lasting change

Our Core Programs

HATA Humanitarian is dedicated to transforming lives by fostering mental well-being, promoting inclusivity, and supporting addiction recovery. Through our holistic approach, we aim to build a community where everyone feels valued, supported, and empowered to reach their full potential.

Stigma-Free & Mental Health | Mindset Education

This program is designed to address the growing mental health concerns among youths in Winnipeg. We aim to create an environment where mental health conversations are normalized, and support is readily accessible.

Key Activities:

  • Mental health awareness workshops
  • Positive mindset and resilience training
  • Peer support and mentorship programs
Learn More

Diversity and Inclusion Program

Our Diversity and Inclusion Program focuses on helping newcomers integrate smoothly into life in Winnipeg. We ensure they feel welcomed and equipped to build successful lives in their new community.

Key Activities:

  • Language training to enhance communication and confidence.
  • Digital skills training for employability and daily life navigation.
  • Navigating the Canadian Education System for the Newcomer Students.
Learn More

Addiction-Free Program

This program is designed to approach and support drug-addicted youths by addressing their immediate needs and fostering long-term mindset change.

Key Activities:

  • One-on-one and group mindset education sessions.
  • Collaboration with rehabilitation services and mental health professionals.
  • Community outreach programs to build trust and support recovery journeys.

Our Board of Directors

Our work is guided by a passionate leadership team with deep community roots and a commitment to creating change.

Pacific Muhumure

CEO & Director

With diverse experience in education and project management, Pacific is committed to empowering youth through education, advocacy, and mindset training, inspired by his own journey as a young refugee.

Angelique Nantabara

Community Leader | Advocate

For over 17 years in Winnipeg, Angelique has been a passionate advocate for youth, newcomers, and individuals with disabilities, drawing from her own lived experience as a refugee from the Democratic Republic of Congo.

Gentille Ntwayingabo

Youth Advocate | Community Builder

Immigrating from the DR Congo, Gentile has called Winnipeg home for over 16 years, a journey that ignited her passion for empowering youth and newcomers. Drawing on her deep lived experience, she works to help others overcome barriers, driven by a belief that every young person deserves the chance to thrive.

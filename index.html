About Us

At the heart of our work is a belief that every person, regardless of background or circumstance, deserves the opportunity to thrive with dignity, resilience, and purpose. HATA Humanitarian Inc. exists to bridge the gaps in mental health care, addiction recovery, and newcomer integration.

Our Vision

An inclusive community where every individual is empowered to thrive with resilience, dignity, and purpose.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower and transform lives by promoting mental well-being, fostering inclusivity, and supporting addiction recovery through education, advocacy, and community engagement. Whether it's providing mindset education in schools, helping newcomers acquire life skills, or supporting youth on their recovery journey, our work is guided by compassion, evidence-based approaches, and a commitment to equity.

Our Core Programs

HATA Humanitarian is dedicated to transforming lives by fostering mental well-being, promoting inclusivity, and supporting addiction recovery.

Stigma-Free & Mental Health

Our flagship program, Stigma-Free and Mental Health, is designed to address the growing mental health concerns among youth in Winnipeg. Through workshops, seminars, and peer support groups, we equip youth with the tools to build emotional resilience and fight the stigma around mental health.

Learn More →

Diversity and Inclusion

Our Diversity and Inclusion Program focuses on helping newcomers integrate smoothly into life in Winnipeg. We offer language training, digital skills workshops, and guidance on navigating local systems.

Learn More →

Addiction-Free Program

This program is designed to approach and support drug-addicted youths by addressing their immediate needs and fostering long-term mindset change. Through one-on-one and group mindset education sessions, and collaboration with rehabilitation services and mental health professionals, we provide a holistic approach to recovery.

Our Board of Directors

Our work is guided by a passionate leadership team with deep community roots and a commitment to creating change.

Pacific Muhumure

CEO & Director

Pacific Muhumure is a dedicated professional with a passion for community empowerment. He is pursuing his second-degree program at the University of Manitoba and holds a degree from Southern New Hampshire University, along with certifications from Harvard University, Boston University, and the United Nations. Pacific has collaborated with RLOs, Government Agencies, and the Ministry of Education to serve the underserved. His diverse experience as an Academic Advisor, Monitoring and Evaluation Officer, Teacher, and Digital Facilitator has fueled his passion for community engagement and advocacy. As a former refugee who has faced immense challenges, Pacific is dedicated to sharing his journey and teaching the importance of mental health and mindset education to inspire young people to unlock their full potential.

Angelique Nantabara

Community Leader | Advocate

Angelique has been a dedicated community leader and advocate in Winnipeg for over 17 years. Originally from the Democratic Republic of Congo, she brings her lived experience to her work, supporting youth, newcomers, and individuals with disabilities. Her extensive experience includes working with various organizations focused on settlement services and community development. Beyond Canada, Angelique has been involved in global community development, demonstrating her commitment to a compassionate and culturally informed approach. Through her work, she has inspired and empowered countless immigrants across Canada.

Gentille Ntwayingabo

Youth Advocate | Community Builder

Gentille Ntwayingabo immigrated to Canada from the Democratic Republic of Congo and has proudly called Winnipeg home for over 16 years. Her journey has inspired her lifelong passion for empowering others, especially youth. A graduate of Golden Bell High School, Gentile is currently pursuing a degree in Business Administration. Alongside her studies, she has worked extensively in community development and support. Gentile brings deep lived experience and heartfelt commitment to her work, understanding the barriers young immigrants face and dedicating herself to helping others overcome those hurdles. Through her involvement in youth programs and newcomer services, she strives to uplift and inspire the next generation.

About Us

At the heart of our work is a powerful belief: that every individual, regardless of their background or circumstances, deserves the opportunity to thrive with dignity, resilience, and purpose. Founded in Winnipeg, HATA Humanitarian Inc. exists to bridge the gaps in mental health care, addiction recovery, and newcomer integration—ensuring no one is left behind.

Empowering and Transforming Lives

Our vision of an inclusive community where every individual is empowered to thrive drives all that we do. Through targeted programs that address mental well-being, combat stigma, and foster inclusive support systems, we aim to create lasting transformation not just for individuals, but for entire communities.

Our mission is to empower and transform lives by promoting mental well-being, supporting addiction recovery, and facilitating social inclusion. We achieve this through education, advocacy, and strong community engagement.

Whether it's providing mindset education in schools, helping newcomers acquire life skills, or supporting youth facing addiction with food assistance and recovery mentorship, our work is guided by compassion, evidence-based approaches, and a commitment to equity.

Our flagship program Stigma-Free and Mental Health | Mindset Education equips youth with the tools to build emotional resilience and fight the stigma around mental health. Through dynamic workshops, peer-led support circles, and hands-on training, we foster environments where healing and growth can take root.

  • Vision: An inclusive community where every individual is empowered to thrive with resilience, dignity, and purpose.
  • Mission: To empower and transform lives by promoting mental well-being, fostering inclusivity, and supporting addiction recovery through education, advocacy, and community engagement.
Objectives:
  1. Promote mental well-being and break the stigma surrounding mental health through mindset education and support programs.
  2. Facilitate the integration of newcomers into Winnipeg by providing essential skills training and support services.
  3. Support youth struggling with addiction by offering food assistance and mindset education for long-term recovery.
  4. Build a supportive network that encourages resilience, personal growth, and social inclusion.

Our Story

Founded by former refugees, HATA Humanitarian transforms lived experience into community empowerment.

Transforming Lives Since 2015

HATA Humanitarian Inc. was born from the lived experiences of our founders who overcame adversity, identity struggles, and the challenges of starting anew in Canada. We bridge gaps in mental health care, addiction recovery, and newcomer integration—ensuring no one is left behind.

7+ Years of community advocacy and youth leadership
Guided by lived experience in migration and resilience
Vision of inclusive, stigma-free communities
Holistic approach to community support

  • Positive mindset training

  • Digital skills workshops
  • Education system navigation
